Inhabitants of the Island of Salsette

A detailed sketch depicting three women from the Island of Salsette, India. The composition is set in a rustic stone environment, capturing a moment of rest and contemplation among the women, dressed in traditional attire and carrying woven baskets. The meticulous shading and textural detail bring life to the figures and surroundings, creating a sense of cultural and historical depth. This piece highlights the everyday life and resilience of the local inhabitants, rendered with an observant and sensitive artistic eye.
